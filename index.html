  • Offer Decisioning and Email Notification

    In the "Offer Decisioning and Email Notification" project, I played a central role in conceptualizing, designing, and implementing advanced UI/UX schemas and audience segmentation using Adobe Experience Platform (AEP). Leveraging AEP’s robust capabilities, I created detailed customer profiles by integrating data from multiple sources, enabling hyper-targeted audience segmentation and personalized marketing strategies. My work involved crafting dynamic customer journeys that aligned with user behavior and business objectives, ensuring a seamless and impactful user experience.

    A key highlight of this project was the development of highly customized and interactive email templates using Adobe Journey Optimizer (AJO). These templates were meticulously designed to cater to diverse audience needs, incorporating dynamic content blocks, real-time data rendering, and adaptive layouts. I utilized Figma extensively to prototype and iterate on designs, focusing on creating templates that were not only visually stunning but also responsive across various devices and platforms. Advanced features such as embedded interactive elements, tailored call-to-actions, and multi-lingual support were integrated into these templates to enhance engagement and cater to a global user base.

    In addition to design, I was deeply involved in ensuring technical excellence. I conducted rigorous unit testing to validate email template performance, including load times, rendering accuracy, and adherence to accessibility standards such as WCAG. I also optimized workflows by automating processes for template generation, ensuring scalability and reducing manual intervention. To document and streamline the process, I created detailed end-to-end workflow guides that served as a reference for future iterations.

    Through this project, I demonstrated my ability to bridge creative design with technical execution, delivering a robust solution that enhanced user engagement and aligned seamlessly with business goals. My expertise in AEP, AJO, and Figma, coupled with my dedication to user-centric design, contributed to a significant improvement in campaign performance and user satisfaction.
